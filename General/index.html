About Pius Omene

Family Law: Pius Omene is dedicated to helping clients navigate complex issues related to separation, divorce, parenting arrangements, and the division of family property. He provides compassionate guidance during these difficult times, ensuring that his clients’ rights and interests are protected throughout the process.

Wills and Estates Planning: Planning for the future is one of the most important decisions anyone can make. Pius Omene guides clients through the intricacies of estate planning, from drafting wills to setting up powers of attorney and representation agreements. He also assists with probate and ensures that the transition of assets to the next generation is handled smoothly and in accordance with his clients' wishes.

Canadian Immigration Law: Pius Omene has extensive experience in handling various aspects of Canadian immigration, including applications for temporary and permanent residence, appeals, leave, judicial reviews, and refugee applications. He is committed to providing thorough and effective representation to individuals and families navigating the Canadian immigration system.

Corporate Law: Pius Omene also offers legal services in corporate law, providing counsel on business structures, contracts, compliance, and other matters crucial to the success of businesses. He works closely with business owners to ensure that their legal needs are met with precision and professionalism.
